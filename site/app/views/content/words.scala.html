Words about Database Flow
  • Here's some words about Database Flow, maybe a slogan or something.
  • An opinionated modern database interface.
  • Running locally or on a shared server, Database Flow helps you discover and search your database from a browser.
  • Database Flow is self hosted, so your data always remains private.
  • The big advantages DBF has over it's competition:
    • - Installed locally or on a server, DBF doesn't need internet access.
    • - Websocket-driven communications, for zero-latency and persistent connections.
    • - Full retina support, and a Material Design implementation that scales from a phone to dual 4K monitors.
    • - Supports the most popular half dozen databases, and adding more is easy (for me).
    • - Explain plan is visualized in a pretty novel way.
    • - Uses the best charting library, webgl/svg support.
  • Database Flow was created by a solo developer. I'm always looking for new ways to improve the product, and would love any feedback you can provide.
  • Marketing is my Achilles' heel, so I'd particularly love some input on how to effectively sell and market Database Flow.
